Ham and Cheese Potato Dinner

Description:

This ham and cheesy potato dinner is a super-easy slow cooker recipe with few ingredients and is truly comforting.

Ingredients:

  • Cooking Spray
  • 28 Oz Frozen Diced Potatoes with Peppers and Onions, thawed
  • 2 cups Shredded Cheddar and Monterey Jack Cheese
  • 1 (10.5 Oz) Can Condensed Cream of Celery Soup
  • 1 (8 Oz) Container Sour Cream
  • 1 (3 lb) Boneless Fully Cooked Ham, halved lengthwise

Directions:

  1. Coat slow cooker with cooking spray.
  2. Combine potatoes, Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese, cream of celery soup, and sour cream in the prepared slow cooker; place ham on top. Cover slow cooker.
  3. Cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours. Stir well before serving.
